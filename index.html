The Problem

Every DMO Knows AI Is Here.
Almost None Have a Plan For It.

It's not a question of whether to adopt AI anymore — boards and partners are already asking. The real questions are how, when, and with what budget: who owns it, what the first move actually looks like, and how to fund it without carving out a full-time hire.

No One Owns It

AI strategy falls to whoever's already stretched thinnest — if it's owned at all. There's no time to track a landscape that changes monthly, let alone build a plan around it.

No Clear Starting Point

Staff experiment individually with ChatGPT or Copilot with no shared workflow, no governance, and no sense of what doing this right actually looks like for a DMO.

No Budget Line For It

AI wasn't in last year's budget, and nobody's sure if the right move is a small pilot or a full platform commitment — so it waits for a cycle that never comes.
